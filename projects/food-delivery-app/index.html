Food Delivery App associated with Food Supplier Link to heading

Introduction Link to heading

This is a food order system based on Java Swing. The System includes farm, food company, the branches of the companies, and normal customers can order food. Within this digital realm, customers can explore menus, place orders, and savor a variety of culinary delights. Farms, companies, and their branches, in turn, manage their offerings and branches efficiently, creating a harmonious link between the agricultural source and the end consumer.

The food order system consists of several components Link to heading

  • Farm: The origin of raw food products, where farms provide fresh ingredients to food companies.
  • Food Companies: These companies receive the products from farms, process them, and offer ready-to-eat or packaged food items.
  • Branches: Food companies may have multiple branches, each managing its own menu and serving local customers.
  • Customers: Customers explore menus and place food orders through the system, choosing from a variety of available items.

Technical Overview Link to heading

The app is built using Java Swing for the graphical user interface (GUI). This allows users to interact with the system through buttons, menus, and lists. Key functionalities include browsing menus, adding items to the order, and finalizing the purchase.

  • Swing for GUI: The graphical interface is designed using Java Swing components such as JFrame, JButton, JList, and JTextArea to create an interactive and user-friendly environment for customers.
  • Order Management: When customers select items from the menu, they can add them to their order list and view the total cost before confirming the order. The system calculates the total price based on selected items.
  • Branch & Company Management: Food companies and their branches can modify their menus, update availability, and track orders from customers.
