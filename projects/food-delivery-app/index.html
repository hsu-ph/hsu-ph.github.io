Food Delivery App associated with Food Supplier

Introduction Link to heading

This food order system, built using Java Swing, provides a comprehensive platform that connects farms, food companies, and their branches with customers. The system allows customers to browse menus, place orders, and enjoy a wide variety of culinary delights. In turn, farms and food companies efficiently manage their offerings, while ensuring a smooth and seamless link between the agricultural source and the end consumer.

Key Components of the Food Order System Link to heading

The system is made up of several key components that work together to provide an efficient and seamless food ordering experience:

  • Farms: These are the sources of raw food products. Farms supply fresh ingredients to food companies, ensuring high-quality, farm-to-table offerings.
  • Food Companies: These companies receive the ingredients from farms, process them, and offer ready-to-eat meals or packaged food items. They manage the product offerings available to customers.
  • Branches: Larger food companies may operate multiple branches, each responsible for its own local menu and inventory. Branches ensure that the products are available to customers within their geographic areas.
  • Customers: Customers explore menus, select food items, and place orders through the system. The user interface makes it easy for customers to browse, add items to their cart, and finalize their orders.

Technical Overview Link to heading

The application is built using Java Swing for the Graphical User Interface (GUI). This allows for a smooth and interactive user experience. Key functionalities of the system include browsing menus, adding items to the order list, calculating the total cost, and finalizing the purchase.

  • Swing for GUI: The app is designed with Java Swing components such as JFrame, JButton, JList, and JTextArea, which create an intuitive and user-friendly interface for interacting with the system. These components enable customers to interact with the app through simple point-and-click actions.
  • Order Management: As customers browse the menu, they can select food items and add them to their order list. The system automatically calculates the total cost based on the selected items, allowing customers to see a summary of their order before confirming the purchase.
  • Branch & Company Management: Food companies and their branches can manage their menus, update product availability, and track customer orders. This functionality ensures that each branch maintains an accurate and up-to-date list of offerings.
